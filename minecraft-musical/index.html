Minecraft the Musical

Act 1. Scene 1.

An actor playing the part of The Menu stands at the front center of an otherwise empty stage. The actor playing the part of The Menu holds up a large sign that looks somewhat like the title menu in Minecraft.

The Menu: (Saying things like the Minecraft menu always does) As seen on TV!

The Menu: Limited edition!

The Menu: Now with difficulty!

Minecraft Steve enters stage left and walks uncertainly toward The Menu.

The Menu: Bringing home the bacon!

Steve: Excuse me, sir.

The Menu: I am the menu.

Steve: Um, your sign says Minecraft. Is this how I get into Minecraft?

The Menu: Absolutely no memes! This isn't a sign. Don't you know I'm The Menu?

Steve: The Menu?

The Menu: Yes, the menu to Minecraft. Gateway to adventure! Trail marker of all things awesome!

Steve: (says nothing)

The Menu: I can tell you're confused. You want to play Minecraft, right?

Steve: Yes, that's right!

The Menu: First you have to pick a name for the world I'm going to create for you. Then you have to decide if you are going to play in Survival Mode or on Peaceful. Then I'll generate a world for you and you can play.

Steve: Uh, I'm sorry, I've never played before. I don't understand.

The Menu: Newcomer, eh? I'll make this easy for you. Say, "I want my world to be named 'Wow! The Menu is super awesome!'"

Steve: (stares at The Menu. He doesn't understand.)

The Menu: (Repeating himself, trying to be patient) Say, "I want my world to be named 'Wow! The Menu is super awesome!'"

Steve: (After hesitating) Uh, I want my world to be named 'Wow! The Menu is super awesome!'

The Menu: Done! Now, do you want to play Survival or Peaceful?

Steve: Uh...

The Menu: Do you want there to be enemies? Do you want to get blown up by creepers?

Steve: Well, I don't know about creepers, but yeah, I think it'll be more fun with enemies.

The Menu: Ok! Generating world. You might want to have a seat, this'll takes a minute. I'm not allowed to show you how I make the world, but I am going to sing to you about it.

Steve: You're going to sing to me?

The Menu: Don't you know anything? This is musical theater! It's got to have songs! Don't worry, I'll take care of this first one. You might want to take some notes.

The Menu sings "In the beginning". A song about creating a Minecraft world.
